# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). By participating you agree to abide by its terms.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to **Niclas Lindstedt** via [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/niclaslindstedt/zig/discussions) or by opening a private advisory at [github.com/niclaslindstedt/zig/security/advisories/new](https://github.com/niclaslindstedt/zig/security/advisories/new).

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The privacy and security of the reporter will be respected at all times.
